Jontelle



"Motherhood is love"

Motherhood Motherhood is love, love when you hear your childs first cry, love as you hold your baby close,love when they take their first breath! Motherhood is guilt, guilt for going to work, guilt for your child going to day care, guilt inside asking “am I doing a good job”? Motherhood is being proud, proud of all their first achievements, proud of their school results and proud of myself-for trying my hardest to be the best mother I can be!.


Tatiana



"I love being a mum"

Motherhood Being a mum you experience all the emotions you can imagine, it’s exhausting, but rewarding at the same time,. It’s a journey that you realised you’re never alone, even if you want to and it’s a feeling that only mum’s know. There are times as a mum you want to run away from your child because he/she is the reason why you’re going crazy but however you cant imagine life without them. I love being a mum. I got pregnant at the age of 22 without a stable job, I didn't chose to be a mum at that age but I learned so much from it. Once I saw the positive test I said to my self that the only option I had was to be strong because from that moment a life depended on me and it was a blessing. To conclude I would like to say to all mums you are a warrior and you should be proud of yourself!.
